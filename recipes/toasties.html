Ham & Cheese Toasties

Description

Two slices of brown bread, buttered on the outside. Four slices of ham with Cheddar cheese on the above and below the ham.

Pre head a George Foreman grill and place the sandwich in with buttered sides of the bread facing outward so that they come into contact with the grill.

Ingredients

  • Two slices brown bread.
  • Four slices ham.
  • Two slices cheddar cheese.
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Pre heat George Foreman.
  2. Butter brown bread.
  3. Place a slice of cheddar cheese on both slices of bread.
  4. Place two slices of ham and both slices of cheese.
  5. Put the sandwich together, butter facing outwards.
  6. Place sandwicch in grill for 4-5 minutes.
