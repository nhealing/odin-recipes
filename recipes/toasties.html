Ham and cheese toasty

Description

Take two slices of white bread and butter the outside of each slice. Add two cuts of ham and one slice orange cheddar cheese. Place the panini-sandwiche into George Foreman Grill or into frying pan with buttered side facing outward. Grill on each side for approximately two minutes or until bread is golden/toasted. Add garlic powder and small pinch of salt to the bread while it toasts.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices white bread
  • Orange cheddar cheese
  • Sliced ham
  • Garlic powder
  • Salt
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter two slices white bread
  2. Place two slices ham and one slice cheese on unbuttered side
  3. Place buttered side out on to George Foreman or frying pan
  4. Add garlic powder and salt to outer side of sandwich
  5. Grill for 2 minutes on each side
  6. Remove when golden brown and enjoy
