Lasagna

Description

Baked cheesy lasgna and just 105 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoon olive oik
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 medium, diced onion
  • 1/2 bell pepper
  • minced garlic clove
  • 3 ounces tomato paster
  • 2 tablespoons oregano
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sugar

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook bell pepper, onion and garlic and add to beef
  4. Add tomatoes and sauce ingredients
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Assemble the lasagna
  7. Bake at 375 for 45 minutes
  8. Let cool and serve
